Common Types of Cancers

Breast Cancer

What is breast cancer?

  • Forms in the cells of the breasts.
  • Can occur in women and rarely in men
  • Breast cancer in women under 40 is relatively uncommon

Breast Cancer

What are the symptoms?

Symptoms include:

  • Breast pain
  • Change in shape of the nipple
  • Nipple discharge or turning in
  • Changes in the size or shape of the breast
  • New lumps or thickening in the breast, especially if in only one breast
